This project has been developed originally to provide solutions to some of the problems faced by farmers viz. access to expertise, crop health management, disease detection and farm management.

The website has been developed with built in AI functionalities to diagnose the condition of the crops, provide expertise and counselling for farmers.

This website acts as an interface between farmers, experts, real time data and the AI models to provide quick and accurate response to the farmers.
